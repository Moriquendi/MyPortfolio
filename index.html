THINGS I DO

Farm 2 iOS

This game was my first iOS project I did in High School. I was stunned with the beauty of Objective-C, Cocoa and the strong community.

Karate Panda

Karate Panda is a PC game for kids I developed when I was 16. Day by day, for over a year, combining my little knowledge of C++ and OpenGL, I finished the it. The game was later published by 'Play' and sold in a few Europe countries.

Base CRM

I had a great privilege to work on the early versions of the Base iOS app. It helps companies to manage their contacts, deals and so much more. The team has been strongly focused on delivering the best experience in the Post-PC era by completely rethinking how enterprise software should work.

Smart Taps

Smart taps is an educational game for small kids. It was build by me and my friend in few days as a warm up before future projects.

Global Game Jam

Athletes goes to gym and hackers goes to hackathons.
Pushing own limits and coding under high time pressure is extremely important. That's why I attended many hackathons. One of them was Global Game Jam during which I made an iOS game in less than 48h.

WWDC

There is no such place as WWDC. Thousands of passionate people brought together always results in thrilling experience. I've attended WWDC in 2012 and 2013 (as Apple's Student Scholarship winner).

CocoaHeads
